The Logic Matrix Programmer


The Logic Matrix Programmer (LMP) is the primary tool for Factorization.
It contains a repository of software, and the data it caries is important and ubiquitous.
Being a vast trove of of data, an LMP can not be built from scratch. They can often be recovered from ancient & unused devices.
Certain socket parts can be configured by clicking on them with the LMP.
Certain Servo Instructions can be configured by clicking on them with the LMP.
