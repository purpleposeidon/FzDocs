The Pressurizer


The pressurizer is a socket tool. It blows entities. Similar to the Lacerator, it must be placed on a motor.
An LMP can configure whether it sucks or blows, and how fast it operates. Faster speeds require more charge to operate.
If it is blowing, it can drops items from a backing inventory, and if it is sucking, it will suck items into the inventory.
When spinning at high speeds, it is able to move players.
A redstone signal will disable the pressurizer.
