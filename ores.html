Ores and Materials


Galena can be found in largish clumps no higher than 46 meters above bedrock. Both silver and lead can be extracted from it. Silver is used to reflect sunlight, and lead is used as a conductor. Furnaces smelt galena into only silver. However, if smelted in a Slag Furnace, it will produce both silver and lead ingots.
